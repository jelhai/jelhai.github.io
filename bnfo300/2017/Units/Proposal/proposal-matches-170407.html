Suggested Proposals to Read
Spring 2017 

I suggest that you read at least one research proposal from past years and compare it to the advice given in How to Write a Research Proposal. Try to find examples of where the proposal adheres to the advice and examples where it does not. Be prepared to discuss your findings with a group of colleagues on Tuesday, April 11.

I've suggested one proposal you might enjoy in the table shown below. However, you are free to explore other proposals. You can get the full list of past proposals by going to VCU Review Panel on Molecular Biology Research for the years 2012, 2013, 2014, and 2016 (there was no 2015).
