Feedback Form - Annotation

BioCyc relies on the collective expertise of those in the field to improve the evidence-based annotation of genes and proteins in its databases. If you see something wrong or lacking, please indicate below how the page can be improved, along with the evidence underlying the suggestion.

Note that as of now, only Tier 1 and Tier 2 databases (not Tier 3) have a mechanism that allows modification of the pages. You can tell the tier of an organism by going to that organism's summary page (e.g. by clicking the name of the organism. You will see at the top, just under the organism's name "Tier X...", where X is 1, 2, or 3.

If you wish to send an attachment, please do so by an email to biocyc-support@ai.sri.com.
