CyanoCyc: Annotation improvement

How to report problems or suggest improvements to annotation

It will not be unusual for you to run across a protein page where there's something important missing or plain wrong. You may well know more about that protein than anyone in the world... Please let us know! With your input, the problem can be fixed, and others may benefit.

Have in hand the assertion you think will fix the problem along with a citation to support the assertion. It would also be helpful if you describe the experiment in a short phrase, like "phenotype of mutation" or "RT-PCR".

To submit the suggestion, you'll find at the bottom of every page in BioCyc either this button:

or a link:
Report Errors or Provide Feedback
Clicking the button or the link will bring you to a page where you can describe what you want to suggest. The form fills itself in with the page where you're calling from, so the human on the other end will already know that.

You'll get an e-mail from a human soon.
